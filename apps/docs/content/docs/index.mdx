title: Introduction
description: What Varys is.
---

**Varys** checks that your app still *looks* right. You capture a flow through your app once,
and Varys replays it — on demand or on a schedule — takes screenshots at the points you marked,
and compares them to a version you approved. Anything that changed is shown to you to **approve**
(it's the new normal) or **reject** (a bug to fix).
